# **App Name**: Sanad Legal Pro

## Core Features:

- AI Legal Co-Pilot: An AI-powered tool that provides legal drafting, contract analysis, and real-time citation extraction in both English and Arabic.
- Intelligent Case Management: Unified dashboard to track legal proceedings, case status, and historical data with automated Firestore synchronization.
- Smart Document Analyzer: AI tool for PDF summarization and OCR document processing with secure enterprise storage.
- Unified Court Calendar: Centralized reminder system for court sessions and deadlines with real-time push notifications.
- Multi-Tenant Team Workspace: Enterprise-grade role-based access control allowing law firms to collaborate across teams while maintaining audit logs.
- Bilingual Interface Engine: Modern RTL and LTR support allowing seamless toggling between English and Arabic professional interfaces.
- Case Intelligence Tool: Uses reasoning to automatically generate chronological case timelines and summarize complex legal activity histories.

## Style Guidelines:

- The design utilizes a sophisticated dark color scheme inspired by 'Deep Trust'. The Primary color is a professional Steel Indigo (#819FD9). The background is a heavily desaturated, dark shade of the same hue (#15171C). The accent color is a bright, analogous Sky Cyan (#66C6D9) for subtle highlights.
- Headline font is 'Space Grotesk' to convey a technical, forward-thinking legal-tech persona. For long legal blocks and data-rich screens, 'Inter' is used as the primary body font to maintain high legibility and neutrality.
- Linear-inspired stroke icons with consistent 1.5px weighting, evoking precision and authority.
- A refined sidebar-first architecture featuring collapsible navigation and wide card-based layouts for dense data tables and legal documents.
- Luxury transitions utilizing Framer Motion with soft springs and subtle glassmorphism fades on modal pop-ups.
